Title

Surya Namaskar Sequence Ontology

Description

Ontology of Surya Namaskar poses, sequence steps, and variants. It models a base SN sequence and three variants, and supports queries about repeated steps, inverse steps, standing support, next or previous steps, and shared poses across variants.
