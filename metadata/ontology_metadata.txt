Title: Surya Namaskar Sequence Ontology

Ontology File: models/combined.owl

Namespace: http://example.org/suryanamaskar#

Format: OWL / RDF/XML

Main Classes:
- Pose
- SequenceStep
- Variant

Main Object Properties:
- hasPose
- belongsToVariant
- hasNextStep
- hasPreviousStep
- repeatsStep
- hasInverseStep

Main Datatype Properties:
- stepNumber
- hasMantra
- hasChakra
- isStandingOnTwoFeet
- isStandingOnAtLeastOneFoot

Author: Mansi Dodiya

Affiliation: IIT (BHU), Varanasi

Description:
This ontology models Surya Namaskar as a sequence of poses, including variants, relationships between steps, and associated attributes such as mantra, chakra, and support type.
